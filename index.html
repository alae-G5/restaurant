Our Story

Built Around One Fire

RENDEZ-VOUS opened with a simple idea: cook everything over real flame and stop hiding behind sauces. No shortcuts, no microwaves — just a wood oven, a flat-top, and a grill running from open to close.

Every dish on our menu is priced the same way we cook it: honestly. Ten plates, one fire, nothing above 150 DH.

"We don't hide behind sauces. We let the fire do the talking."

— Chef Amine, Head of Kitchen
